About S.G.M. Ramesh

S.G.M. Ramesh is a dedicated candidate running for MLA with a strong commitment to improving our community. With a background in S.G.M. Ramesh is a prominent candidate for the MLA position with a strong foundation in both education and public service. He holds advanced degrees in Computer Engineering and an MBA, which provide him with a unique blend of technical expertise and strategic management skills. Ramesh has contested in the MP elections twice in Nagapattinam, showcasing his dedication and deep understanding of the local political landscape. His public service commitment is further emphasized by his family background; he is the son of S.G. Murugaiyan, a pioneering leader and the first Scheduled Caste Member of Parliament from Nagapattinam. Additionally, Ramesh is connected through his father-in-law, A.K.S. Vijayan, who also served as a Member of the Lok Sabha from Nagapattinam. With his rich background and experience, S.G.M. Ramesh is well-prepared to bring impactful and innovative solutions to the challenges facing his constituency. , he brings experience and a vision for a better future.
